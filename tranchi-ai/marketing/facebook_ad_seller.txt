FACEBOOK LEAD AD — SELLER FUNNEL ($20–40 budget)
Paste these directly into Ads Manager. One ad set per state.
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

CAMPAIGN OBJECTIVE:  Leads
DAILY BUDGET:        $10/day (run 3–4 days, then pause)
PLACEMENT:           Facebook Feed only (cheapest cost-per-lead)

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
AUDIENCE (build one ad set per state)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Location:    TX  (then duplicate for FL, OH, GA, NC, TN)
Age:         35 – 65+
Gender:      All
Detailed Targeting:
  • Behaviors → "Homeowners" / "Likely to move"
  • Interests → "Real estate", "For sale by owner (FSBO)"
Exclude:     "Renters" if available

Start with ONE state (TX or FL). Scale the winner.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
AD CREATIVE — Variation A (Speed)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Primary text:
Need to sell your house fast? We make cash offers in 24 hours —
no repairs, no agent fees, no showings. Sell as-is and close on
YOUR timeline. Get your no-obligation offer today.

Headline:        Get a Cash Offer in 24 Hours
Description:      No fees · No repairs · No obligation
Call to action:  Get Quote

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
AD CREATIVE — Variation B (As-Is / pain relief)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Primary text:
House needs work? Inherited a property you don't want? Tired of
being a landlord? We buy houses in any condition, for cash, with
zero repairs or cleanup needed. See what yours is worth — free.

Headline:        We Buy Houses As-Is — Any Condition
Description:      Fair cash offer, no fees, close in days
Call to action:  Get Quote

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
WHERE THE AD POINTS
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Website URL:   https://YOUR-RAILWAY-URL.up.railway.app/sell

(Replace YOUR-RAILWAY-URL with your live domain. Test it first —
visit /sell in a browser and confirm the form loads before spending

Privacy Policy URL (Facebook requires this on every lead ad):
   https://YOUR-RAILWAY-URL.up.railway.app/privacy

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
IMAGE
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Use a clean single-family home photo. Free + commercial-use:
   https://unsplash.com/s/photos/house  (download, no attribution needed)
Avoid stock photos with visible faces or text overlays — they
underperform and can trip Facebook's text-on-image filter.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
WHAT TO EXPECT AT $40 TOTAL
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Cost per seller lead:  $8 – $15
  Leads generated:       3 – 5
  If sold at $75–100 ea: $225 – $500 back
  Watch in Ads Manager:  if a lead costs >$20, pause and switch
                         to the other creative variation.
