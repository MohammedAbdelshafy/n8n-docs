Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

Tranchi AI ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, why, and the choices you have. By submitting a form on this site you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

When you voluntarily submit one of our forms, we collect only what you give us:

  • Your name, phone number, and email address
  • Property address and details you choose to share (condition, timeline, reason for selling)
  • The date, time, and IP address of your submission, plus the consent text you agreed to (kept as a record that you opted in)

We do not collect data about you from third-party brokers, and we do not track you across other websites.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To contact you about a cash offer for your property, or about real estate deals you asked to receive
  • To send the follow-up messages you consented to, by phone, text, or email
  • To keep a compliance record of your consent

We do not sell or share your personal information without your consent. If we ever offer a lead-referral service that shares your details with a vetted home-buyer, it happens only on the basis of the consent you gave when you submitted your information — and you can withdraw it at any time.

3. Messaging & Consent (TCPA)

By checking the consent box and submitting a form, you agree to be contacted at the phone number and email you provided, including by automated text message and calls, about your property or the deals you requested. Consent is not a condition of any purchase or sale. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies.

4. How to Opt Out

  • Text: reply STOP to any message to be removed immediately
  • Email: reply UNSUBSCRIBE, or use the unsubscribe link in any email
  • Request deletion: email us and we will delete your information from our records

5. Data Security & Retention

Your information is stored in an access-controlled database with row-level security. We keep it only as long as needed to serve you and to maintain consent records, and we delete it on request.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your state, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information, and to opt out of its sale or sharing. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the email below.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Email us and we'll respond within a few business days.
